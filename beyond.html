Fertilizer

Organic fertilizers are derived from natural materials such as compost, manure, and plant residues. They provide nutrients to plants slowly over time and help to improve soil quality. Some organic fertilizers may also contain beneficial microbes that can help to improve plant health..

Treshers

Rice threshers typically work by feeding the rice stalks into a chamber where they are beaten or rubbed against a screen. This process separates the rice grains from the stalks and chaff. The grains then fall through the screen and are collected in a bin. The stalks and chaff are expelled from the machine..
